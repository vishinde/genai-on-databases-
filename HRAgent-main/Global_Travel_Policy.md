# Global Corporate Travel & Expense Policy
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Scope:** All Global Employees

## 1. Daily Meal Allowance (Per Diem)
The standard daily meal allowance for business travel is $75 USD per day. This covers breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Receipts are required for any single expense exceeding $25 USD.

## 2. Air Travel
Employees must book the "Economy Restricted" fare class for all domestic flights under 4 hours. For international flights exceeding 8 hours, employees may book "Premium Economy."

## 3. Lodging
The preferred hotel chain is 'CloudStay Suites'. Employees should use the corporate booking portal to ensure negotiated rates are applied.
