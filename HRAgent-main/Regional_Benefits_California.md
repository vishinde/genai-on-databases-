# Supplemental Benefits: California, USA
**Region Code:** US-CA
**Scope:** Full-time employees residing in California.

## 1. Commuter Benefits
California employees are eligible for a monthly pre-tax transit subsidy of up to $150 USD for use on BART, Caltrain, or approved vanpool services.

## 2. Wellness Stipend
In accordance with California health initiatives, employees in this region receive a $500 annual stipend for gym memberships or mental health applications. This is a taxable benefit.

## 3. Work-From-Home (WFH) Equipment
Employees based in CA may request a one-time reimbursement of $250 for ergonomic office equipment, provided they work remotely at least 3 days per week.
