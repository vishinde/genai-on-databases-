# Executive Leadership Policy Addendum
**Applicability:** Levels L8 (Director) and above.

## 1. Business Class Travel
Notwithstanding the Global Travel Policy, L8+ employees are authorized to book "Business Class" for any international flight exceeding 5 hours to ensure business continuity upon arrival.

## 2. Guest Entertainment
Directors and above are authorized to host "Client Development" dinners. The meal limit is increased to $200 per person when entertaining external stakeholders or potential recruits.

## 3. Dedicated Support
L9 (VP) and above are eligible for a dedicated administrative assistant and a monthly executive coaching stipend of $1,000 USD.
